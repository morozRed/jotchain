JotChain helps you show up prepared for every conversation without manual prep.

Log your day in simple notes—no templates needed. Before a meeting, JotChain assembles a focused summary with highlights, context, and recent activity, grounded in your entries. You get the signal without hunting through tickets or chat threads, and you can share the same brief with your manager, team, or clients.
